This release primarily adds support for replying to mastodon and bluesky posts as well as links.


* Add ability to reply to mastodon and bluesky posts by @aciccarello in [#143](https://github.com/omnibear/omnibear/pull/143)
* Adds support for sharing client id metadata so Clients can look up Omnibear's name and logo ([acf5c93](https://github.com/omnibear/omnibear/commit/acf5c9385d1b0b630d98bffc4f070fc72ac5d4d2))
* Updates dependencies ([eaf9d50](https://github.com/omnibear/omnibear/commit/eaf9d50400f79d0406c74e473f022338e6d03869))
* Bump qs from 6.14.1 to 6.14.2 by @dependabot[bot] in [#148](https://github.com/omnibear/omnibear/pull/148)
