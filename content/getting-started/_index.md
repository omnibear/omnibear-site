To use Omnibear, your website will need to support two things: [authentication](authentication) and [micropub](micropub).
These pages will help you get everything set up and ready to go.
Once you have posted to your site, you might (optionally) consider informing other sites with [webmentions](mentions).

**If you're not ready to set up these features on your website, consider trying Omnibear by logging in with [commentpara.de](https://commentpara.de) and it will take care of the rest.**
